Who Am I?


My name is Michael Fragiskatos and I live in Kitchener, Ontario. In the fall of 2019, I embarked on a major career change in my life by jumping into web development. I dabbled in some coding and it made me realize this is what I want to pursue. I completed the Web Development program through Career Foundry where I created projects using frameworks such as React, Angular, and Vue.js. I have since continued practice these skills through new projects while simultaneously learning new skills.

When I'm not coding I enjoy travelling, working out, hot yoga, playing guitar, going to concerts, and binge watching TV series.
